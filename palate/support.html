Quick answers

A menu scan did not work

Use a clear, well-lit photo with the menu text in focus. For online scans, check your connection and try again. You can also select an existing menu image from your photo library.

I need to restore a purchase

Open Settings, choose Palate Premium, then tap Restore Purchases while signed in to the same App Store or Google Play account used for the purchase.

Offline scanning is unavailable

Offline menu interpretation is a premium feature. Download a supported model over Wi-Fi from Settings, Translation Method before traveling without a connection.

I want to delete my data

Favorites, recent scans, settings, and saved API keys are stored locally. Delete them in the app or uninstall Palate. For a server-side usage-record request, email support.

Food safety

Translations and generated dish details can be incomplete or inaccurate. Always confirm allergens, ingredients, dietary suitability, and prices with restaurant staff before ordering.
